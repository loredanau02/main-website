Description

The Starless Sea is a fantasy novel by Erin Morgenstern, published in 2019. The story follows Zachary Ezra Rawlins as he discovers a mysterious book that leads him on a quest filled with magical doors, underground libraries, and timeless stories. The novel weaves together elements of fantasy, romance, and adventure.
